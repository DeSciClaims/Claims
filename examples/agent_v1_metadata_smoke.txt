Metadata Smoke Study

Abstract: In 40 synthetic cases, Treatment B reduced median recovery from
9 days in the control group to 6 days in the treatment group over a 14-day
follow-up. The study used random assignment. Long-term outcomes were not
measured.

Methods: Cases were randomly assigned to Treatment B or control. Recovery time
was measured as the first day on which symptoms were absent.

Results: Median recovery time was 6 days with Treatment B and 9 days with
control.

Limitations: The study was synthetic and did not evaluate long-term outcomes.
